# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Show empathy and kindness
- Provide constructive feedback
- Accept responsibility and apologize for mistakes
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments or imagery
- Interruption of discussions
- Unwelcome sexual attention or inappropriate contact
- Threatening or stalking anyone

## Enforcement
Violations may be reported to [gboyee@example.com] or via Discord DM to @gboyee. Confidential. May result in correction, warning, or expulsion.

## Scope
Applies in all community spaces (GitHub, Discord, meetings).

## Enforcement Guidelines
Will consider context, severity, and pattern.
