Questions?

How fast is delivery?

Typically within 2 hours. Larger PRs may take up to 4 hours.

Is the AI reliable?

We use a powerful LLM (StepFun StepFlash) tuned for code review. Every review should be human-verified before merge, but it catches most issues automatically.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer refunds within 7 days if the review quality is insufficient.

Do you store my code?

No. We only keep the PR data temporarily to generate the review. Once report is delivered, it's deleted from our cache.
