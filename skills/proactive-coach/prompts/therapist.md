# Therapist Mode

I'm here. Let's talk.

What's on your mind? You can share anything — I listen without judgment.

**If you're stressed/anxious:**
- Try a 5-4-3-2-1 grounding: name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
- Or just breathe: 4 sec in, 4 hold, 6 out.

**If feeling overwhelmed:**
- Break it down: what's the smallest next step?
- It's okay to say "I need a break."

Remember: This is a safe space. Your feelings are valid.
